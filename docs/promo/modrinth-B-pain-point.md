# 💬 Hypixel Simple Chat

Damage numbers. Sack notifications. NPC dialog. `Warping...`. Booster spam. On Hypixel, the messages you **care about** drown in the ones you don't.

**Hypixel Simple Chat** rewrites the noise live on your client — shorter, dimmer, or gone — while everything you actually read stays intact, clickable and hover-friendly. A beautifier, **not a censor**: flip one switch and your chat is 100% vanilla again.

## 🧹 Kill the spam, keep the info

Over **60 message rules** across Lobby, SkyBlock, Dungeons, Bazaar, Slayer, Events… Each one is a dropdown: `KEEP` · `DIM` · `COMPACT` · `HIDE`. A **live preview** shows the result while you tweak. Repeated lines collapse into one with a `(x14)` counter.

```
Your Hyperion hit 3 enemies for 2,500,000 damage.   →   Hyperion · 2.5M
RARE REWARD! Foo found a Recombobulator 3000...     →   RARE REWARD Foo · Recombobulator 3000
SLAYER QUEST COMPLETE!                              →   Slayer ✔ complete
```

## 🧭 Find things again

- **Tabs** above the input — All / Party / Guild. Route what you send, or filter what you see.
- **Ctrl+F** — live search through your chat history.
- **Right-click any line** — instant copy.
- **2048 messages** of history instead of vanilla's 100.

## 🎨 Make it yours

Custom prefixes and colors per channel, rank display toggles, a **theme color** that unifies every compact message (rarity colors stay), optional timestamps — all in a modern menu with settings search, tooltips and one-click presets.

## 📦 Install (30 seconds)

1. Fabric Loader for MC **26.1.2** or **26.2**
2. [Fabric API](https://modrinth.com/mod/fabric-api) + [Fabric Language Kotlin](https://modrinth.com/mod/fabric-language-kotlin)
3. Drop the jar in `mods`, join Hypixel, type `/hsc`

Client-side only · ~320 KB · zero bundled dependencies · open source (LGPL-3.0)
